About Me

I am a former biomedical scientist who has transitioned into a role as a refugee case worker at a charitable organization dedicated to supporting refugees and asylum seekers as they integrate into society. In my daily responsibilities, I am deeply involved in assisting clients in their quest to find suitable educational institutions and housing while offering them unbiased information and guidance.

My role encompasses a diverse range of tasks, with a primary focus on facilitating access to essential welfare support and financial benefits, particularly for individuals who may not be well-acquainted with the intricacies of the system. Beyond my professional commitments, I hold a profound passion for technology and its potential to bring about transformative changes in people's lives.

My introduction to Code Your Future occurred during my research into services that could benefit our clients, and I quickly realized that it resonated perfectly with my personal values and aspirations. Assisting refugees in rebuilding their lives has long been a deeply ingrained goal of mine, and I view coding as the ideal avenue to accomplish this mission.

I am driven by a strong desire to acquire programming skills and employ them to create a meaningful and positive impact. My commitment to perpetual learning remains unwavering, and I intend to harness my background as a refugee case worker to excel in the technology sector, contributing to the betterment of both individuals and communities.
