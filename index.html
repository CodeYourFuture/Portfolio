About Me

I am a biomedical scientist turned refugee case worker at a charity supporting refugees and asylum seekers in their integration into society. My daily tasks involve aiding clients in finding suitable educational facilities and housing, as well as providing independent information and guidance. My role is multi-faceted, with a focus on facilitating access to welfare support and financial benefits, especially for those unfamiliar with the system. In addition to my work, I am passionate about technology and its potential to transform lives. I discovered Code Your Future while researching services for clients and found it perfectly aligned with my values. Assisting refugees in rebuilding their lives has always been a personal goal, and coding is my dream avenue for achieving this. I'm eager to learn programming and apply my skills to make a positive impact. Committed to continuous learning, I plan to leverage my experience as a refugee case worker to excel in the tech sector.
