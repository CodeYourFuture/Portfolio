About Me

I'm Rabia, a Manchester resident since 2017 and a proud mother of two. My background is rooted in architecture, where I developed a passion for design and precision.

After years in the field, I've embarked on a new journey as a full-stack developer student at CODE YOUR FUTURE. I'm immersed in the world of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and more.

My primary drive is to build a stellar portfolio and secure a meaningful role in the tech industry. Each line of code I write fuels my ambition to contribute to innovative solutions. I believe in the endless possibilities that the tech landscape offers and am committed to continuous growth and learning.

My family's support and the CODE YOUR FUTURE community inspire me on this exciting path. I'm eager to explore how I can make a lasting impact on the ever-evolving tech world.
